Africa in Motion.
Stories That Connect the World.

ARRIVAL Magazine is an African aviation, travel, and lifestyle publication dedicated to telling the continent's stories through movement, culture, innovation, and global connectivity.

Our Story

ARRIVAL launched in 2005 at a defining moment in Nigeria's aviation and tourism growth, positioning itself at the intersection of travel, business, and lifestyle. Over nearly two decades, the brand has expanded beyond print into a content-rich digital ecosystem while maintaining strategic distribution across Nigeria's major airports and international hubs. With offices and partnerships spanning Lagos, Nairobi, and Accra, ARRIVAL reflects a truly pan-African outlook.

From Print to Digital Excellence

Founded in 2005 with a clear vision — to present Nigeria and Africa as destinations of excellence, opportunity, and world-class experiences. What began as a premium print publication has evolved into a dynamic digital platform serving a global audience.

Early editions gained visibility in airport lounges, embassies, and executive spaces, quickly establishing the publication as a trusted voice within aviation and hospitality circles.
